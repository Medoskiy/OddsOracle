Web Crawler Engine
Our crawler fleet of 14 distributed nodes hits all 14 major sportsbooks simultaneously every 60 seconds. We use a rotating proxy pool to avoid blocks, and smart parsing algorithms to extract odds data from each book's JavaScript-rendered pages.
14 sportsbooks 60s refresh rate 14 proxy nodes 9,800+ markets/scan
2
⚖️
Odds Normalization & Market Mapping
Raw odds from each book are normalized into a standard decimal format. We apply vig removal algorithms to strip the bookmaker's margin from each outcome, giving us the "fair" implied probability for every market. Events are matched across books using our proprietary event matching engine.
Vig removal Event matching Market normalization
3
📡
Signal Collection — 6 Key Factors
For each event, we collect and score 6 predictive signals that our model weights independently. These signals are calibrated based on historical accuracy data across 50,000+ past events.
1. Recent Form (5 games) 22%
2. Head-to-Head Record 18%
3. Home/Away Advantage 16%
4. Sharp Line Movement 20%
5. Injury Report Impact 14%
6. Public Money Fade 10%
4
🤖
Claude AI Analysis
Each event is passed to Claude (Anthropic's AI model) with a structured prompt containing all 6 signal scores, the normalized odds, historical context, and injury data. Claude generates a sharp analyst narrative and provides a clear BET / MONITOR / PASS recommendation.
Claude AI 3-sentence verdict BET / MONITOR / PASS Confidence grade A/B/C

💎
Value Bet Detection (+EV)
The model compares our calculated true probability against the market's implied probability. If our model gives Team A a 65% win probability but the best available odds imply only 52%, that's a +13% edge — a clear positive expected value (+EV) situation flagged as a VALUE BET.

✓ PRO TIP: Focus on Grade A

Grade A picks (confidence ≥83%) have historically outperformed Grade B and C picks by a significant margin. Start by filtering your predictions to Grade A only.

💡 Use Value Bets for ROI

Simply picking winners isn't enough for long-term profit. Focus on VALUE BETS (orange ◆) where our model finds a genuine edge vs the market odds. These provide positive expected value over time.

⚠️ Bankroll Management

Never bet more than 1-5% of your bankroll on a single event, even on Grade A picks. Variance is real. The Kelly Criterion suggests 2-3% for strong +EV bets.
